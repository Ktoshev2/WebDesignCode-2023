Musical Picture Gallery


This page is a gallery of pictures involving both my experience with the piano, and the books I used over time:


The Giant Book of Intermediate Classical Piano Music was my first proper music book.

I had used basic exercise booklets before, but this book got me into actually fostering musicality.


The Well Tempered Clavier, a set of two baroque pieces for all 24 major and minor keys, allowed

me to begin working on my technique and musicality simultaneously in a more sophisticated way.


The current music book I use is nothing more than a binder full of printed out pieces which I learn.


This is a picture of me and my teacher right after a recital. The pieces I performed at this specific recital were

Rachmaninoff's Prelude in C Sharp Minor, Chopin's Nocturne in C Sharp Minor,

Chopin's "Revolutionary" Etude, and Ravel's "A la maniere de Emannuel Chabrier".
